Why It Matters

  • Flooding is the most common nautral disaster in the United States and unfortunately, Pennsylvania has the highest rate of flooding in the nation.
  • As Pennsylvania's infrastructure continues to age, in conjunction with monumental change in the globe's climate, it is now more crucial than ever to address the issue of flooding and tackle it with all resources at our disposal.
  • The situation is especially precarious in Pennsylvania, specifically Philadelphia, which is one of the largest cities in the nation. In the southern part of Philly, for instance, as that area is regarded as the most prone to flooding or a water-related emergency.
  • High amounts of rainfall contribute to this problem.
  • Many areas that flood in Philadelphia are lower-income parts of the city.
  • Not enough is done to properly tackle the issues with the infrastructure of the city that is sorely in need of an update to keep up with changing times
